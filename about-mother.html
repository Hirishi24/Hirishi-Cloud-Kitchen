Satya Saladi
Founder & The Heart of Hirishi Cloud Kitchen
Satya Saladi's journey began in her home kitchen, where her love for authentic, traditional flavors blossomed into a passion for sharing homemade goodness with the world. With every recipe, she brings the warmth, care, and tradition of generations, creating food that feels like a hug from home.

As a mother and a visionary, Satya's mission is to deliver not just food, but memories and love to every household. Her dedication to quality, hygiene, and the true taste of home is the foundation of Hirishi Cloud Kitchen.
Every recipe I create is a piece of my heart, made with love and tradition. I want to share the authentic taste of home with everyone.
